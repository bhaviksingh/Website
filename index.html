Bhavik Singh is an artist and technologist who imagines slow, intimate futures for social software. He makes art as soft networks and works on product at Patreon.

Past experiences include work at BeReal and Google, membership at New Inc, a fellowship with the Processing Foundation, and teaching at The Cooper Union School of Art and Hunter College.

He enjoys biking through Prospect Park, watching birds through windows, and cooking daal for friends. He is very happy you stopped by and would love to chat more.
